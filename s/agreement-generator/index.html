Frequently Asked Questions

Is this agreement legally valid?

The format generated is legally correct. However, for legal validity, you must print it on appropriate stamp paper and get it signed by both parties with witnesses.

Will the stamp image appear in print/PDF?

No, the stamp image is only for visual preview. PDF and print output will not include the stamp image as per legal requirements.

Do I need to fill Father's name and Address?

Yes, these are mandatory for legal agreements. Father's name and address help establish proper identification of the parties.

Can I change the font size?
