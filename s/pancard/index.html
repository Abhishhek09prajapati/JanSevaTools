What is the NSDL PAN card photo size?

NSDL PAN card photo size is 276x197 pixels (3.5x2.5 cm) with maximum file size of 20KB at 200 DPI. The background should be white or light colored.

What is the UTI PAN card photo size?

UTI PAN card photo size is 213x213 pixels square format with maximum file size of 30KB at 300 DPI. Must have white background only.
