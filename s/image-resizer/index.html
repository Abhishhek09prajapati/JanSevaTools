Frequently Asked Questions

How to resize image to exact KB?

Switch to "Resize by KB" mode, enter your target size (e.g., 100 for 100KB), and click resize. Our tool automatically finds the best quality to achieve that file size.

Is this image resizer really free?

Yes, completely free! No hidden charges, no registration required. You can resize unlimited images.

What's the maximum file size?

You can upload images up to 20MB. For KB resize, target size can be up to 5MB (5120 KB).

Will my images be secure?

All processing happens in your browser. Images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I maintain aspect ratio?

Yes, in dimension mode you can lock aspect ratio. When you change width, height automatically adjusts to maintain proportions.
