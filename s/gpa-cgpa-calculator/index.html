Frequently Asked Questions

How is GPA calculated?

GPA = (Sum of (Grade Points × Credit Hours)) ÷ Total Credit Hours

How to convert CGPA to percentage?

Multiply CGPA by 9.5. Example: 8.2 CGPA × 9.5 = 77.9%

Can I save my data?
