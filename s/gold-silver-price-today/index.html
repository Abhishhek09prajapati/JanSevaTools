Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I add multiple jewellery items at once?

Yes! The Multi-Item Jewellery Cart allows you to add unlimited gold and silver items. Each item can have different weight, purity, making charges, and GST. The cart calculates individual prices and shows a combined grand total.

2. How is the price calculated for each jewellery item?

For gold: (Rate per 10g ÷ 10) × weight × purity factor (24K=1, 22K=0.916, 18K=0.75) = metal value. Then add making charges % and GST %. Silver follows similar logic with KG rate ÷ 1000 × purity factor (999=1, 925=0.925).

3. Can I use different making charges for different items?

Absolutely! Each item in the cart has its own making charges dropdown (0% to 20% for gold, 0% to 12% for silver) and GST selection (0% to 18%). This reflects real-world jewellery purchases where different designs have different making charges.

4. How do I check today's live gold and silver prices?

Click the "Gold Silver Price Today" button at the top of the page. It will open a Google search showing the latest gold and silver rates in India. You can then enter those rates in our calculators for accurate cost estimation.
