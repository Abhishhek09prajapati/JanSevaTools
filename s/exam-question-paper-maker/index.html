Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is this exam paper maker really free?

Yes, this exam question paper maker is completely free! No registration, no hidden charges, no credit card required. You can create unlimited question papers for any subject and class.

Can I create question papers in Hindi language?

Absolutely! You can switch between English and Hindi languages using the language toggle button. All labels, instructions, and section headings will be translated automatically. Your question text remains as you type.

How to download the question paper as PDF?

Simply click the green "PDF" button after creating your question paper. The paper will be downloaded as a professionally formatted PDF file with perfect A4 layout, ready for printing or sharing.

Can I add both MCQ and descriptive questions?

Yes! You can add both types of questions. MCQ questions appear in Section A with options, and descriptive questions appear in Section B with answer spaces. Question numbers continue sequentially across both sections.

How to hide general instructions from the paper?

Use the "Hide Instructions" toggle switch in the left panel. When enabled, the instructions section will be hidden from the preview, print, and PDF output. This gives you more space for questions.

What are the benefits of using this exam paper maker?

This tool saves time for teachers, ensures professional formatting, supports bilingual education, provides instant PDF download, and is completely free. It's trusted by over 10,000 teachers across India.
