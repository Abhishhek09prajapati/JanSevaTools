Frequently Asked Questions

How to get A4 layout?

In download section, select "A4 Layout" option. The tool automatically calculates how many photos fit on one A4 sheet.

Can I add colored border?

Yes! Choose from white, black, gray, gold, blue, or any custom color. Adjust border thickness from 1-20px.

What is the standard size?

India/UK: 35x45mm, USA: 2x2 inch, Canada: 50x70mm. All presets included.

Is this really free?

100% free! No watermarks, no signup, no hidden charges. Create unlimited photos.
