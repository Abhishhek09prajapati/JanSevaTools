How to Use the Free Name Slip Maker Online

Creating professional and beautiful school name slips has never been easier. Designed specifically for CSC operators, Cyber Cafes, and parents, this tool by JanSeva Kendra Tools allows you to design, arrange, and print perfectly measured name slips directly onto A4 paper.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  • Select the Theme: Choose the 'Boy' template (Yellow) or the 'Girl' template (Purple) based on preference.
  • Upload Image: Click the upload box to add a passport-sized photo. Our tool ensures facial features remain clear and centered without distortion.
  • Add Cartoon (Optional): Upload a transparent PNG sticker of a cartoon and use the sliders to place it exactly where you want it.
  • Fill Details: Type the Student's Name and School Name. Use the "Switch to Hindi" button to change labels (Class, Section, Roll) to Hindi (कक्षा, वर्ग, रोल).
  • Print or Save: Click "Direct Print A4" to instantly print 10 perfect slips on a single page, "Save as PDF" for sharing, or "Download Single PNG" to save just one sticker.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Will the slips cut off while saving as PDF?

No! We have mathematically coded the A4 dimensions. When you click "Save as PDF", all 10 slips will fit perfectly inside the A4 boundaries without getting cut from the bottom.

2. Is my uploaded photo safe?

Yes, 100% safe. The images are processed locally in your web browser. No data is sent or saved on our servers.

3. How do I get transparent cartoon images?

You can download any free transparent PNG cartoon from Google Images and upload it here to customize the name slip further.
