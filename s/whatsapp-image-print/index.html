Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Smart ID Scanner tool.

Is my data safe?

Yes, absolutely. All image processing happens locally in your browser. We never upload your sensitive ID cards or documents to any server.

Can I scan any document?

Yes! While optimized for ID cards (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID), you can use the "Single Document" mode for any certificate, letter, or one-sided document.

How do I get the best print quality?

Ensure your photos are taken in good lighting and are not blurry. Use the "Magic Enhance" filter to sharpen text and adjust brightness/contrast if needed.

What formats are supported for download?

You can download your scanned results as high-resolution JPEG images or as a ready-to-print PDF file.
