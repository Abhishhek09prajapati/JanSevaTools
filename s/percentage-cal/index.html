Formula: [(200-150)÷200] × 100 = 25%

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate percentage of a number?

To calculate X% of Y, multiply Y by X and divide by 100. For example, 20% of 150 = (20 × 150) ÷ 100 = 30.

How to calculate percentage increase?

Percentage increase = [(New Value - Original Value) ÷ Original Value] × 100. For example, from 100 to 150 is a 50% increase.
