Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is an affidavit and when is it needed?

An affidavit is a written sworn statement of fact voluntarily made by an affiant under an oath. It is needed for name change, address proof, date of birth correction, marriage registration, passport applications, and various legal proceedings.

Is this affidavit generator free to use?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use. You can generate unlimited affidavits without any charges. No registration or login required.

Do I need stamp paper for affidavit?

Yes, affidavits typically require non-judicial stamp paper of appropriate value (₹10, ₹20, ₹50, or ₹100 depending on state). This tool helps you visualize the stamp paper in preview, but you must purchase actual stamp paper from licensed vendors.

Can I upload my own stamp paper image?

Yes, you can upload your own stamp paper image using the "Upload Custom" button. The image will appear in preview only and not in the final PDF or print.

Is the generated affidavit legally valid?

The format generated is legally correct. However, for it to be legally valid, you must print it on actual stamp paper and get it notarized/attested by a notary public or oath commissioner.

What is the stamp paper value required for affidavit?

Stamp paper value varies by state. Generally, ₹10 stamp paper is sufficient for most affidavits. Some states require ₹20, ₹50, or ₹100. Check your local state stamp act for exact requirements.

Can I use this affidavit for court purposes?

This affidavit format is suitable for general legal purposes like name change, address proof, etc. For court-specific affidavits, consult a legal professional.
