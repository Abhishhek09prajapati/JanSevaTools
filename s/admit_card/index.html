Frequently Asked Questions

Is this admit card manager really free?

Yes, it's completely free with unlimited card creation. No registration or payment required.

Can I download admit cards?

Yes, each admit card can be downloaded as a text file. You can also export all cards at once.

Where are my cards saved?

Cards are saved in your browser's local storage. They remain even after closing the browser.

Can I edit cards after creating?

Yes, you can edit any card anytime using the edit button on each card.
